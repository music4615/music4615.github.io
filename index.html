I am a senior student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University (NTUEE). I am currently an undergraduate research member in the Multimedia Processing and Communications Lab (MPAC) led by Professor Homer H. Chen. I also work in the Image and Vision Lab under the supervision of Professor Yi-Ping Hung in the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering.

My research interest is in digital signal processing with a concentration on video/audio processing. I am particularly interested in any research related to music. My undergraduate research on "Music Recommendation System," which is based on artist novelty and similarity, helps users to find artists who are not yet famous and achieves good performance. Together with two classmates, we built a "Mind Map" product prototype to record and organize participants' flow of minds during brainstorming. You can find more information about my undergraduate research and projects in Projects.
